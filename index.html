Ice Cream

Ice cream is a delightful frozen treat that everyone likes. In the oldern days, ice cream was sold in a truck that comes down a street playing a song to catch your attention. Some places still have ice cream trucks but nowadays, usually you can only get it in a store or at an ice cream shop. According to https://www.icecream.com/icecreaminfo, 87% of America has ice cream in their freezer at any time.

Why It Is Called Ice Cream

A long time ago, people used ice to cool down sweet cream or custard in order to make ice cream. It was named "iced cream". It then changed into "ice cream" in 1744.

The History

Nobody really knows who really made ice cream.

Kinds Of Ice Cream

There are many different kinds of ice cream. A few most known ones are mochi, gelato, soft serve, and sherbert.
